gender: the individual's biological sex
age: the individual's age in years
hypertension: indicator variable for hypertension, a condition that causes blood pressure in the arteries (0 for no, 1 for yes)
heart_disease: indicator variable for the presence of underlying heart disease (0 for no, 1 for yes)
smoking_history: never, former, or current smoker
bmi: the individuals' body mass index
HbA1c_level: Hemoglobin A1c level, a measure of the individual's average blood sugar level over the past 2-3 months
blood_glucose_level: the amount of glucose in the individual's bloodstream at a given time
diabetes: indicator variable for diabetes (0 for no, 1 for yes)
